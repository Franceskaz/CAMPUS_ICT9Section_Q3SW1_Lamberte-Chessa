Gap The Series: Pink Theory




Sypnosis:

Gap The Series is a Thai GL (Girls Love) video uploaded to YouTube by Idol Factory. It is based on the novel "GAP" by Devil Planoy. The plot revolves around two girls, 'Mon' (Becky Armstrong) and 'Sam' (Freen Sarocha Chankimha).

Sam is a well-known wealthy lady in Thailand. She is well-known for her friendliness, and Mon has long been in love with her. Mon naturally becomes an intern at the same company where Sam is the CEO after she graduates from university. However, when she arrives at the company and meets Sam on the first day of her internship, she discovers the truth about her being rude.

Characters

"Sam" Samanan Anantrakul

Samanan Anantrakul, also known as "Khun Sam," is a main character in the series. She is a wealthy woman descended from the Thai Royal family. She owns a business, but her employees describe her as rude and arrogant. She is also Kirk's 'fiancee,' whom her grandmother arranged for her when she was young. Despite being arranged, a girl named 'Mon' applies to her company, and it is there that Khun Sam falls in love with her.

"Mon" Kornkamon

Kornkamon, also known as "Mon," is a main character in the series. She is a friendly person who enjoys the color pink. She is also well-known for her devotion to Khun Sam. She looked up to Khun Sam because when they were kids, Sam saved her from being hit by a car. So she pursued Khun Sam for years until she applied for a job at her company, where she discovered Sam's true colors. However, after becoming acquainted with Khun Sam, she falls in love with her instead of idolizing her.

Kirk

Kirk is the antagonist/supporting character in the series. He is known to be Khun Sam’s finance in a marriage arranged by Sam’s grandma. In Khun Sam’s company, he is known to be the “kind boss” as he gives exceptions to the employees in the company. Though not because of his attitude he is hated by much of the fan base. #kirksucks
